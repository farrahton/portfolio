UNLEASH app

It seeks to eliminate poor connectivity, boost the creative process, and protect intellectual property of artists around the world. By using cutting edge Artificial Intelligence and 5G Connectivity, UNLEASH fosters a creative environment through virtual technology for future generations of musicians.

De Gezonde Stad campaign

The client De Gezonde Stad wanted a solution to motivate people from the Tropentuin to start growing vegetables and herbs themselves. We live in the Netherlands and we all know that the weather is not always nice. Playing in the mud in a vegetable garden or tropical garden can then be less attractive. At such moments, the outdoor activities of the rest of the campaign do not match the needs of the target group very well. I have a solution for that, an indoor activity: a board game!

HvA Pride campaign

With a quiz campaign I want to respond to the fact that the target group spends a lot of time on the internet. They consume a lot of media entertainment. Often times these topics are not discussed in school. Yet this media has a lot of influence on young people. So for a quiz with a pop culture theme they can apply their knowledgde for a giveaway. Moreover, it is a great way to show how much representation there is in the media and that we are aware of the students' interests. The sentence they have to guess is ’We involve everyone.’ (with the 1 standing for one), which also sends the message that the HvA encourages inclusivity.

It is often said that it is too difficult a subject to discuss among children. This need not be the case. There are 13 guiding principles that I would like to introduce to children in the form of a quartet game.
