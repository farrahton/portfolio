Instagram carousel

Creating content to raise awereness for important and sometimes relatively unknown topics is something very dear to my heart. This is an Instagram carousel about ARFID, which is a new nutritional disorder and has been included in the psychiatric manual DSM-5 since 2013. The abbreviation ARFID stands for Avoidant/Restrictive Food Intake Disorder. With this eating disorder you are not consciously concerned with your weight, but you refuse to eat food with taste, color of texture. This is difficult for yourself and those around you. You may try to feel weak and dizzy because you don't eat enough to actively exercise, with no plans of losing any ultimate goals. Often people also with ignorance with "You're just a picky eater". The carousel is part of a Dutch cross medial story.
